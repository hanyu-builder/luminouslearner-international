Almost every substance in the universe is denser as a solid than as a liquid. Water is the weird exception. Ice cubes float in your drink, and lakes freeze from the top down.

This unique property is essential for life on Earth, and it all comes down to the shape of the molecule and Hydrogen Bonding.
